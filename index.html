How to Use the Bird Classifier

  • Click "Choose File" to select a high-quality photo of a bird
  • Ensure the bird is clearly visible and well-lit in the image
  • Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP (maximum 10MB file size)
  • Click "Predict Bird Species" to analyze your image with AI
  • View detailed results with confidence percentage and species information
  • Explore additional images and information about the identified species
  • For best results, use images with the bird as the main subject
