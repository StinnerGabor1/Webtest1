About Us

We're a team of passionate ornithologists, data scientists, and conservation biologists dedicated to making bird identification accessible to everyone through the power of artificial intelligence.

Our mission is simple: democratize wildlife knowledge and contribute to global conservation efforts by providing accurate, instant bird species identification to researchers, educators, and nature enthusiasts worldwide.

Our AI model can identify over 1,486 bird species with 94% accuracy, helping thousands of users connect with nature and contribute to citizen science projects.

Founded in 2023, we started as a small research project and have grown into a comprehensive platform used by over 50,000 users globally. Every image classified through our system helps build a better understanding of bird populations and biodiversity trends.

We believe that technology should serve conservation. That's why we've made our platform free and accessible, ensuring that anyone with curiosity about birds can learn and contribute to scientific knowledge.

Our work is supported by partnerships with leading conservation organizations and research institutions. We're committed to maintaining the highest standards of scientific accuracy while making complex AI technology simple and user-friendly.

Whether you're a professional researcher, a student, or someone who simply loves birds, our platform is designed to enhance your understanding and appreciation of the incredible diversity of avian life on our planet.
